# Privacy Policy for iReport Camarines Norte

**Effective Date:** November 5, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 5, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to iReport Camarines Norte ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you create an account or submit an incident report, we may collect:
- **Name:** Your full name for identification purposes
- **Email Address:** For account creation and notifications
- **Phone Number:** Optional contact information
- **Date of Birth:** To verify age requirements (13+ years)
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates of incident locations

### 2. Incident Report Data
- **Description:** Details of the incident you're reporting
- **Photos/Videos:** Media evidence you choose to upload
- **Timestamp:** Date and time of report submission
- **Agency Type:** Which emergency service (PNP, BFP, PDRRMO) you're reporting to

### 3. Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with the app
- **Network Information:** Connection status for offline mode functionality

### 4. Guest User Data
- **Anonymous ID:** Temporary identifier for guest users
- **Reports:** Incident reports submitted as a guest
- **Note:** Guest data may be lost if you clear app data or reinstall

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Process Incident Reports**
   - Submit reports to appropriate emergency agencies
   - Route reports to nearest stations
   - Track report status and updates

2. **Provide App Functionality**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Enable offline report submission
   - Send notifications about report status

3. **Improve Our Services**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and improve performance
   - Develop new features

4. **Ensure Safety and Security**
   - Prevent fraud and abuse
   - Enforce rate limiting (5 reports/hour)
   - Protect against spam

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We Share Your Information With:

1. **Emergency Agencies**
   - PNP (Philippine National Police)
   - BFP (Bureau of Fire Protection)
   - PDRRMO (Provincial Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office)
   - **Purpose:** To respond to your incident reports

2. **Service Providers**
   - Supabase (Database and authentication)
   - OpenStreetMap Nominatim (Address geocoding)
   - **Purpose:** To provide app infrastructure

3. **Legal Requirements**
   - Law enforcement when required by law
   - Court orders or legal processes
   - Protection of rights and safety

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Share media with third parties except emergency responders

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- **Database:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) - encrypted at rest
- **Media Files:** Supabase Storage - secure cloud storage
- **Offline Queue:** Local device storage (encrypted)

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data transmission
- Secure authentication with Supabase Auth
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Regular security audits
- Access controls for emergency personnel

### Data Retention
- **Active Reports:** Retained indefinitely for record-keeping
- **Resolved Reports:** Archived after 2 years
- **Guest Accounts:** Data retained until manually deleted
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data removed within 30 days

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction:** Update or correct your information
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
4. **Portability:** Export your data in a readable format
5. **Opt-Out:** Disable notifications in app settings
6. **Withdraw Consent:** Delete your account at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights
- **In-App:** Profile → Settings → Privacy Options
- **Email:** privacy@ireport-camarinesnorte.gov.ph
- **Response Time:** Within 30 days

## Children's Privacy

iReport is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

## Location Data

### How We Use Location
- **Incident Location:** GPS coordinates of where the incident occurred
- **Auto-Fill:** Reverse geocoding to provide address
- **Station Routing:** Find nearest emergency station

### Your Control
- Location permission is required to submit reports
- You can manually edit location if GPS is inaccurate
- Location data is only collected when submitting reports

## Offline Mode

When you submit a report offline:
- Report is stored locally on your device
- Automatically submitted when internet is restored
- Local data is encrypted
- Deleted after successful submission

## Third-Party Services

2. **OpenStreetMap Nominatim**
